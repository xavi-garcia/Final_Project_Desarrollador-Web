Hi! Welcome to this little corner in the virtual world! I'm Xavi but you can also call me by my drag persona's name Madame Supreme. I'm an Argentinian artist with a passion for watercolors, makeup and performing. I like to combine my vast experience in painting with my love for drag to create a myriad of otherworldly characters.
