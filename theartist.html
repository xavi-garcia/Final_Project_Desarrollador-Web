ABOUT ME

An new expressionist artform based on pop culture elements inspire a uniquely creative style. My aesthetic is a messy clash of artistic movements. My main inspiration is my queer experienceand also that of other queer folks. I've always conceived it as an otherworthly experience so that's why I'm drawn to the baroque, the gothic but in conjunction to a colorful and inspiring future.

Annie Lenoxserves as a muse to the drag persona Supreme. Lennox is a captivating performer who trascends space ands time. This performance happened in the context of the premiere of Dracula (1992) by Bram Stoker.

"El poder infinito de nosotrxs monstruos" is an exhibition that took place the 15th November, 2019 in Casa de Pepino The paintings exhibited were part of a collection creates by me since 2017. Many of the paintings dealt with trauma and queer identities.

A performance by the drag persona Madame Supreme framed the exhibition. It served as an extension of my artistic askills and my love for performing arts.
